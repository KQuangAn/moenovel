# Getting Started

Before start writing books you need to enroll yourself as an author. You can go to manage section and register from there.

## Steps :-

- **Registration:** Navigate to the navbar and click on the profile menu and go to the manage page.

![Author Registration](/author-reg.png)

- **Next Step:** Click `Become an author` button and fill the necessary details.

- **Final Step:** After registering, you will recieve an email with a secret key copy that and paste it in the verify section.

![Entering secret key](/author-reg-key.png)
